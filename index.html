The Narrative

A Love Tested by Fate

"محبت اور دھوکہ ساتھ ساتھ چلتے ہیں"

"Love and deception walk side by side"

Kamyar — charming, impulsive, magnetic. Ayra — principled, pure-hearted, grounded. Two souls from entirely different worlds find each other in the most unexpected way.

What begins as a sweet, tender connection slowly unravels into a storm of emotions, hidden secrets, and quiet heartbreak. As Ayra discovers the layers beneath Kamyar's surface, she must choose between love and truth — and face the possibility that the person she cherishes most may not be who she believed him to be.

A story of love that dares to ask: how well do we truly know the ones we love?
